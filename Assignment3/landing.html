The Game of Badminton

The Rules

The rules for playing badminton are surprisingly complex, especially if playing at the tournament/professional level. There are many finer points in the rules that most recreational players have never even heard of, such as 'faults' and 'lets'.

Included here are two rulebooks; the first is a more easy to digest, more compact rule set that would satisfy most recreational players. The second is a much more in depth look at the rules of the sport, and covers many more topics, such as equipment weight and size, tournament hand gestures and carding, and shuttle speed. Even most professional players wouldn't know half of the things included in this book, so don't concern yourself too much with memorizing it if you want to start playing.

The Top Players

At the top of the men's list is Lee Chong Wei of Malaysia, who is my personal favorite. His style is much more fun and fluid than most other players, making him a pleasure to watch.
