This is a match between what many would consider the two best players in the world, Lin Dan and Lee Chong Wei. They have had a rivarly for quite some time, with Lin Dan usually coming out on top despite Lee Chong Wei consistently being ranked higher. This was a match played recently, during Lee Chong Wei's remarkable winning streak despite his older age.


A men's doubles match from the 2015 Australian Open. Double's matches are typically faster paced and more intense than mens single's, with more of an emphasis on smash power instead of shot placement.
